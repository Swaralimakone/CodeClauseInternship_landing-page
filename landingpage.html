HOW TO APPLY LIQUID LIPSTICK:

1.Shake for at least 5 seconds before using.Make sure your lips are dry and clean before appplying

2.Apply in the center of your upper lip and follow the contours of your mouth.

3.Glide across the entire bottom lip.

4.Allow product to dry
